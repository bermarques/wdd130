Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as an engaing platform, leading new customers to our service and make it easy for anyone to find new all needed information.

Audience

What do they want?

Most of them will want to do a fun activity while on vacation, and some of them will be experienced water rafters looking for challenges.


What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

Answers about the benefits of rafting, more info on the routes available and locations, and encouragement for the first timers.


How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

We expect that most of them will be accessing through a laptop or a mobile device, and lastly by desktop.
