Rio Grande do Sul

Rio Grande do Sul is a state in the southern region of Brazil. Located in the southernmost part of the country, Rio Grande do Sul is bordered clockwise by Santa Catarina to the north and northeast, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, Uruguay to the south and southwest, and Argentina to the west and northwest.
